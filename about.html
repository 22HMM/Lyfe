About Us

We are a professional team of landscape architect, we offer one year warranty of our products, and giving our customers 100% satisfactory experience is our goal, so why wait? try me!

Lyfe Company Limited is founded in 2014 in Yangon, we're a professional team of landscape architect. Both landscape design and installation service available, our customers include the government, factory, residence, and private property etc.By using modern and reasonable design in our projects, we promise our customers only pay for the least money to do the most things. Thus, we give one year warranty of our products which is built by us or the hardwares we used, living plants are also included, aside from improper care and neglected watering of the plants after handed over to the owner.In this fiscal year, we're about reaching 200 million Ks goal. We have 4 staffs in our East Dagon nursery, and 16 staffs as a mobile team of landscape architect. By the future, we are trying to be success as a leading company in Myanmar landscape architect!
